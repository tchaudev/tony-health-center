Our Doctors

Dr. Tony Ly

Chiropractor

Hello! My name is Dr. Tony Ly and I am delighted to be here in Tustin, CA.

I vividly recall the heartache of watching my brother wrestle with learning difficulties and health setbacks, each missed school day a dagger in our family's hopes. Despite our relentless pursuit of answers, we found ourselves drowning in a sea of uncertainty. Watching his struggle served as a painful reminder of life's fragility and the profound sorrow it can bring.

As I grew up, I faced my own health challenges. I visited hospitals regularly, but tests showed nothing wrong as to why I was having stomach pains. This spiraled into anxiety and depression. Eventually, we discovered chiropractic care, which taught me how the spine affects the nervous system and overall health.

Chiropractic adjustments helped me regain control of my health, bringing joy to my family. Surprisingly, my brother, who has special needs, also benefited greatly from this approach.

Seeing my brother's progress inspired me to become a chiropractor myself, despite having a background in business. It wasn't easy going back to school, but the journey was worth it. I wanted to share the life-changing effects of chiropractic care with others.

As a chiropractor, my passion is working with families and children, focusing on brain-based chiropractic to address health issues from an early age. I've seen amazing improvements in children with ADHD, anxiety, and asthma through chiropractic care. My clients come from all walks of life, and it's fulfilling to help them achieve better health and wellness.

I see myself as a guide, helping people tap into their natural ability to heal. I believe everyone can improve their health with the right support, and chiropractic care can be a big part of that journey.

From being a sick child to becoming a chiropractor, it has been an incredible adventure. I'm grateful for the opportunity to help others transform their lives through chiropractic care. If you're struggling with health issues or worried about a loved one, consider exploring the benefits of chiropractic care. It could make a bigger difference than you ever imagined.
